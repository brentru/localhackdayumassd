Event Details:

Who can participate?

Any student at an academic institution. This includes universities, colleges, and high schools (refer to our faq about high school students). We are not limiting this event to UMass Dartmouth students, any student in Rhode Island or Massachusetts that is interested is welcome!





When is Local hack Day?

Local Hack Day @ UMassD kicks off at 9AMEST, October 10, and last until 9PM EST.





Where is Local Hack Day?

LocalHackDay@UMassD will be at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth in the Science/Engineering Building (SENG), room TBD.

Address:285 Old Westport RoadNorth Dartmouth, MA 02747





What if I’ve never attended a Hackathon?

Local Hack Day is a mini-hackathon! It’s a way for you to have a hackathon experience, but closer to your house! It’s a great way to start and learn some coding skills. Regardless of experience level, all are welcome to come learn, celebrate, build, and compete.

F.A.Q.

What if I'm not from UMassD or any uMass?

We invite all schools (university/college/high schools) to our local hack day. It is a great way to build a working knowledge of coding, and improve on your knowledge by applying it to an awesome project! Local academic institutions in Southern Massachusetts, Rhode Island, and the Massachusetts Region are not only invited, but encouraged, to attend!

I'm a high school student, I can attend right?

Absolutely! Just make sure you have a parent/guardian at the event while you are coding away. They are free to take advantage of our workshops and possibly write a few lines of code during the day!

What can I win?

This event is not about winning. It's about learning and making friends that will last for a lifetime. If you're thinking, "that's adorable..but really what can I win", then we've got you covered too. A panel of judges from the Massachusetts and Rhode Island region will select the best projects from the day based on the following criteria: creativity, technical difficulty, polish (have your ux design kits and nice fonts ready!), and usefulness. Top prizes are TBA.

How much will LocalHackDay at UMassD Cost?

$0.00. Nothing. Nada. Zip. Zilch. Zero. We'll provide the lunch, breakfast, snacks, free swag, and drinks for twelve hours. You just show up, form a team with friends or strangers, and work on a project that you came up with!

I'm not a student. What now?

Well, you're not allowed to participate (sadly). However, we are actively looking for mentors. Mentors are invite-only from the LocalHackDay UMassD Team. We are not allowing mentors to "show-up", you need pre-approval. Get in contact over at mentor@corsaircoding.pw

How do you run this event?

This event is planed by UMass Dartmouth students with advice from Major League Hacking (MLH.io). 100% of the funding comes from sponsor organizations.

What should I bring to this event?

Laptop, any mobile device (or hardware platform) that you may decide to code on, ethernet cable, power strip, charger (DONT FORGET YOUR LAPTOP CHARGER, WE HAVE NO SPARES!). If you have an item you are unsure of, email questions@corsaircodeing.pw Do not bring alcohol, drugs, animals, or weapons. You will be promptly removed from the event and reported to UMass Dartmouth's Department of Public Safety.
